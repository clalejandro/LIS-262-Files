Workshop in PHNOG 2018

By: Christine Alejandro, March 15, 2018

PhNOG 2018 held a 2-day workshop last March 8 and 9 at the I'M Hotel in Makati. The workshop is about Network Monitoring and Management. Jonathan Brewer is the instructor. The participants are technical personnel from universities and telco companies.

On the first day of the workshop, there was an icebreaker. The icebreaker was an online poll on the background of the participants. It was a fun activity to get to know the other participants.

The topics discussed on day 1 were introduction to network monitoring and management, Simple Network Management Protocol (SNMP), log management and Smokeping.

On the second day of workshop, the topics discussed were configuration management, ticketing systems and LibreNMS.

There were hands-on exercises during the workshop. The class were divided into several groups. The instructor had set up virtual routers which were used during the workshop.

Overall, it was a productive 2-day workshop. For sure, the participants have learned a lot from the discussion and hands-on exercises.
